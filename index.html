WHY US?

We will not get into the usual bit about quality and such like matters. We will start with our size. We are small enough to value every customer that we work with and large enough to give them what they require. Our size gives us flexibility in manufacturing. So, lead times, product mix, customisation are all on the table. As we said, we work with our customers. Which brings us to the next point. We see ourselves not as mere vendors but as partners. We partner our customers because it is in their success that we find our success. Therefore, we are solution oriented. You need a specific cable? We will develop it for you. You need a cable with modified specifications? We will manage that to the best of the cable's operating parameters. You need to cut manufacturing time? We will try our best to do so. Basically, once you have us working you can focus on what you best and leave the cables to us.

GREEN BHANSALI

As we have progressed along our manufacturing journey over the seven plus decades that we have been around, the environment has come into sharp focus. In fact, it is the state of the environment that bothers us all and we too have adopted greener manufacturing processes and procedures. As a result, RoHS compliances are mandatory and the provenance of raw material becomes all that more important. We take care to minimise waste generation and also its safe disposal. After all, we are all creatures of our environment and if we do not take care of it, who else will?

About us

Our story starts over seventy years back. From a modest beginning cut to today. Bhansali is a recognised brand name in the industry offering a comprehensive line of cables and wires. How did we get there? We evolved as the cable TV industry grew in India. From standalone operators to large MSOs and the advent of DTH, Bhansali cables played a small part in carrying all the action to the home. As the telecom sector boomed Bhansali cables carried bits and bytes for leading telecom service providers in the country. The growth of the power sector in India has seen electricity flow through Bhansali cables. Lighting up residential complexes, powering industrial units and keeping offices humming with activity. And, as the power sector has increased its generation from renewable sources, Bhansali has been present there as well. Defence and security has been in the news of late. This sector too has seen the presence of the Bhansali brand. But, as you may understand, we can't say anymore! This is just a sample of the industry/sector coverage from Bhansali cables. All in all, whatever be the sector or industry, chances are there is a cable from Bhansali to meet your needs and requirements.
