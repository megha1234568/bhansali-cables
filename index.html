About us

Bhansali Group has been in existense in India for last six decades. Established in the year 1940 i.e. the pre-independence era the group has seen new heights every year by enhancing its manufacturing technology and manpower skills. Today the company manufactures Radio Frequency Coaxial cables, Equipment wires, Instrumentation & Data cables, Control cables, Telephone cables, Local Area Network cable, CATV cables. Available from a single source all our products are designed to meet the needs of most critical installations for safe and reliable transmission of voice, video and data. From the beginning customer service has been of utmost importance to us. As we strongly believe in the sovereign position of the customer, we provide quick and efficient service which ensure customer satisfaction. To accomplish this we have a separate unit for customer care that is responsible for taking into account every query given by the customer, which is resolved as quickly as possible.
