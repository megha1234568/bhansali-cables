GREEN BHANSALI

As we have progressed along our manufacturing journey over the seven plus decades that we have been around, the environment has come into sharp focus. About us

And, as the power sector has increased its generation from renewable sources, Bhansali has been present there as well. Defence and security has been in the news of late. All in all, whatever be the sector or industry, chances are there is a cable from Bhansali to meet your needs and requirements.

Manufacturing edge

We produce our cables from our two manufacturing units in Delhi and Bhiwadi in the National Capital Region. ISO 14001:2004 and ISO 9001:2000 certified, our manufacturing facilities and processes are flexible enough to handle orders for routine, running cables as well as custom-made cables.

We produce our cables from our two manufacturing units in Delhi and Bhiwadi in the National Capital Region. ISO 14001:2004 and ISO 9001:2000 certified, our manufacturing facilities and processes are flexible enough to handle orders for routine, running cables as well as custom-made cables. Our products are put through IMI, in-process and final strenuous testing procedures in our well-equipped testing laboratory. Meeting delivery deadlines is top priority for us since we understand that any delay in the delivery can contribute to overall project delays and cost overruns. Our cables are supplied in wooden reels, corrugated boxes and coils. Cable ends are sealed with BOPP self-adhesive tape and non-hygroscopic sealing caps to protect them from moisture. We know that our future depends on our ability to develop new products and processes and to optimise performance of existing products. Towards this end, our R&D goals are: • Develop new products and new manufacturing processes; • Monitor and remain abreast of emerging technologies • Anticipate customer demand. Our R&D focus is on faster innovation and reducing time-to-market.
